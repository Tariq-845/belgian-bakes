Rijstaart

Savoury rijstaart, also known as Dutch rice cake, is a traditional dish from the Netherlands. It is typically made with cooked rice mixed with eggs, cheese, and a variety of savory ingredients such as vegetables, herbs, and spices. The mixture is then baked until it forms a firm and golden crust. Savoury rijstaart is a delicious and versatile dish that can be served as a main course or as a side dish, and it is perfect for both casual meals and special occasions.

Methods

To make savoury rijstaart, start by preheating your oven to 180°C (350°F) and lightly greasing a baking dish. In a large bowl, mix together 2 cups of cooked rice, 4 eggs, 1 cup grated cheese, 1 cup diced vegetables, and 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh herbs. Season the mixture with salt and pepper according to your taste. Transfer the mixture into the prepared baking dish, making sure it is spread evenly. Bake in the preheated oven for about 40-45 minutes, or until the top is golden and the rice cake is set in the center. Allow the RIJSTAART to cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve. It can be enjoyed both hot and cold, and pairs well with a fresh salad or as a side dish with roasted meats.

Ingredients

The ingredients for Rijstaart are as follows:

  • Ingredients:
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup grated cheese (such as Gouda or Cheddar)
  • 1 cup diced vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, mushrooms, onions, or peas)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh herbs (such as parsley or thyme)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
